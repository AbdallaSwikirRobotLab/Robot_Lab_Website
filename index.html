About AIRoCS

We advance intelligent robotics and control with an emphasis on safe learning, aerial manipulation, vision-language navigation, SLAM on embedded platforms, and multi-robot swarms. Our work spans theory, algorithms, and deployment on real systems.

Featured Research

We merge AI and robotics to create safe, adaptive control strategies, designing intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and operate reliably in complex environments.
